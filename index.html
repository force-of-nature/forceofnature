Services

Fully trained in all aspects of caring for your garden, we have a wealth of horticultural experience after years working alongside New Zealand's top landscape designers and within many award winning gardens. Whether a one-off tidyup, redevelopment, getting a property market ready, a redesign or on-going garden care, Force of Nature Gardens are 'your garden specialists'.

Plant selection

Plant selection & Nutrition

Force of Nature Gardens will assist with suitable plant selections to enable them to flourish in their desired location and ensure your garden succeeds in reaching it's full potential. We are fully trained in the nutritional requirements of a variety of plants and we will manage these for your peace of mind.

Pruning and training

Pruning and training

Many plants and trees require specific pruning techniques to keep them in optimal health, at the desired shape or height, produce flowers and bushy thick growth or produce fruit. Incorrect pruning and a lack of training can result in reduced crops, increased risks of pests and diseases and even plant failure/death. We are skilled in the various techniques required and want to see your garden succeed.

Attracting wildlife

Attracting birds, bees, butterflies and other wildlife

Force of Nature Gardens is passionate about the environment here on the Hibiscus Coast and the surrounding Auckland area. Being blessed with local bird sanctuaries, residential gardens are the perfect place to encourage native birds and butterflies using specific trees and shrubs, which offer nectar (important for bees too), berries and shelter. We will assist in selecting, planting and caring for these important food and shelter plants and turn your property into a flourishing oasis.

Lawn installation and care

Lawn installation and care

Lawn areas are an important design feature and require skill and technique to keep in constant good health for both use and aesthetics. Force of Nature Gardens will install, renovate and maintain top lawn standards, managing pests, diseases and weeds.

Space conscious design

Space conscious design

Stunning designs and green training techniques can be used to accentuate or disguise walls and fence lines. Areas which capture excessive heat over summer and appear hard and life barren can be transformed into a living feature or extend existing sculptural designs used in a building's architecture.

Edible and fruiting plants

Edible and fruiting plants

Grow your own supplies of fresh vegetables, herbs and superfoods and eat straight off the plant. Have your food grown your way, avoiding nasty chemicals used in commercial growing. Invest in plants and avoid expensive food prices while helping reduce transport carbon footprints.

Hedge trimming and health

Hedge trimming and health

From plant selection through to regular fertilising, FORCE OF NATURE Gardens will prune and train the best hedges possible making them a great investment in your property's desirability as well as increasing privacy and aesthetic appeal. All too often hedges are left to creep out or up and get to a stage when extreme, unsightly cutback jobs or costly removal and replant is required. To avoid this contact 'Your Garden Specialists'.

Creating a stylish entrance

Creating a stylish entrance

Correct plant selection to compliment a property's architecture and design is a key element in creating an entrance that leaves a lasting impression. Accentuate your property by adding small trees, shrubs, hedges or climbers to add instant grandeur, heavenly scents, colour or seasonal interest. Create an experience for the senses from the gate, the driveway or in pots by the front door.

Lush 'Outdoor Rooms'

Lush 'Outdoor Rooms'

Outdoor areas can easily be transformed into 'Outdoor rooms' enhancing the indoor-outdoor flow. Perfect for entertaining on warm, clear nights or simply relaxing in your own tranquil oasis. The use of fragrant plant species is another way to enhance your garden experience.

Potted plants

Potted plants

Our team can ensure your potted plants are kept healthy and add aesthetic value to hard surfaced areas, or grow plant specimens until large enough to put in the garden. Potted plants are a great feature which can be relocated when areas require maximisation of space, to hide service areas such as pool sheds and equipment or rubbish bin storage areas.

About

Logan, the 'force' behind Force of Nature Gardens has over 15 years experience in the Horticulture and Landscape industry as well as a personal passion for nature and the environment. In previous roles Logan has worked with Australasia's top Landscape designers, numerous award winning residential landscapes, on Bird sanctuaries and Environmental projects and NZ's largest Greenwalls and green roof projects as well as studying tropical plants in Melbourne and Sydney. Logan is a registered horticultural trainer and also makes time to educate children about the environment and has shared his knowledge of bees and their vital role in a number of local schools and pre schools. Having grown up on the Hibiscus Coast and now sharing this amazing place with his wife and four children, he intends to inspire this same appreciation and respect for the outdoors in our future generations.
